Remarks:

This is a Chi-spuare test for independence. One assumption is the expected frequency for each category must be 5 or more.

The P-value (or probability value) is the probability of getting a sample statostic or a more extreme statistic in the direction of the alremative hypothesis when the null hypothesis is true.

Guidelines for P-Values:
For example:

H0 = injury severity is independent of the type of injury

H1 = injury severity is dependent of the type of injury (claim)

The hypothesis test is a right-tailed test
It calculated P-value = 0.25, then do not reject null hypothesis. There is not enough to support the claim H1 that injury severity is dependent of the type of injury.
